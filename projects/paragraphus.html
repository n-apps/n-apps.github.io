Paragraphus — Android app

2020 · Android app · Live on Play Store

Paragraphus helps readers discover new books through anonymous excerpts. Users swipe through random passages — if something catches their interest, they can reveal the book and author. If not, they simply swipe left to move on.
My role

I built Paragraphus as a collaboration exploring new ways to discover content. I led the full process — from concept and design to development, testing, and publishing.
